Everything you need to know about Global SIP Trunking

Session Initiation Protocol (SIP) is a digital way to make and receive phone calls and other forms of cloud-based communication over an Internet Protocol (IP) network. Communications platforms like virtual phone systems, unified communications, and contact center software, use SIP to talk to one another over the internet. Organizations and consumers alike use SIP to create, manage, and terminate calls (or sessions).
