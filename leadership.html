Leadership

BRCK was initially made for the harshest environments on earth, now we’re bringing that same level of attention to the US market.

In 2013 BRCK was founded with a mission to build the most reliable internet routers in the world. Since that time, we have grown to not only build the world’s most reliable routers, but also some of the best software and communications equipment to reliably keep people, businesses, and organizations connected and talking around the world. BRCK has a long history and a strong foundation in building resilient and reliable communications, and we are focused on building towards “what’s next”, bringing real innovation into this industry.

Lance Condray

Chief Executive Officer

Lance has extensive experience as an entrepreneur and senior executive driving technology and telecom strategy and growth. He's held leadership roles renowned companies such as Bandwidth, Cytracom, OmniNET, and Facebook. With a strong focus on developing connectivity solutions and products, particularly for rural and emerging markets, Lance has made significant contributions in the field. Lance was Bandwidth's first CFO in 2001 where he led the team that built Bandwidth's enterprise business unit. Lance's entrepreneurial journey began with the founding of Endaga, a startup that later caught the attention of Facebook, leading to its acquisition. As an executive at Facebook and BRCK, Lance has played a pivotal role in leading connectivity and infrastructure projects, including the notable TIP and OpenCellular initiatives. With extensive experience in technology and telecom strategy, Lance is well-equipped to support BRCK's customers and establish the company as an industry leader. 

T.R. Misser

Chief Technical Officer

T.R. began his 30 year career within the telecommunications sector in 1994, commencing with a position at GTE located in Tampa, Florida. Subsequently, he demonstrated continued excellence and growth working with US West and Hewlett Packard in Colorado during the 90s. In 1999, he joined Level(3) Communications, where he was pivotal in the development of the industry’s first softswitch. This formative experience provided the foundation for his profound comprehension of telecommunications infrastructure.

From 2006 to 2010, T.R. held various positions at Bandwidth ending his time there as Chief Technology Officer, a testament to his technical proficiency and leadership abilities. During his tenure at Bandwidth, he architected and implemented Bandwidth’s first SIP Trunking solution. Additionally, he led the establishment of Bandwidth’s CLEC (Competitive Local Exchange Carrier) network, solidifying their status as leaders in the telecommunications domain.

In pursuit of sustained growth and innovation, T.R. assumed the role of lead architect for Dialpad after helping found Plivo as first employee after the co-founders. Over a span of five years at Dialpad, he immersed himself in the intricacies of the Enterprise Telephony realm, thereby further augmenting his expertise. Subsequent to his tenure at Dialpad, T.R. joined Google, where he assumed leadership of the Voice and Messaging Operations team on a global scale.

In a return to his passion for working with small business, T.R. has reentered the realm of SIP Trunking. As our Chief Technology Officer at BRCK, he is committed to drawing upon his decades of experience and innovation to support our current and prospective customers. In conjunction with his leadership role at BRCK, T.R. cultivates his passion for technology as the founder of VoipJedi Inc., where he persists in exploring and challenging the boundaries of telecommunication.

T.R. holds a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of South Florida, attended the University of Colorado Boulder, Master’s degree in Telecommunications program and is a veteran of the United States Marine Corps where he worked in Aviation Weather until the end of Operation Desert Storm.
