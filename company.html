Welcome to BRCK -
your trusted partner in global communication.

BRCK was established in 2013 with a mission to solve complex communication challenges. The company was born out of the need for reliable communication in rural Africa, where the infrastructure was often days away from any sort of civilization. The ethos of the company is built on the principle that technology should be resilient and reliable, especially when it is mission-critical.

Our Mission

BRCK empowers global teams with connectivity through resilient and innovative communication solutions

We are committed to solving complex challenges, enhancing customer experiences, and ensuring every voice (or message) is heard, no matter where it originates."

Our Values

  1. Reliability and resilience come first, ensuring communication solutions work when needed most.
  2. We offer a real human touch, with responsive and customized support. BRCK has real people that deeply understand voice communications and provide white-glove service.
  3. With a leadership team that has shaped the VoIP industry, our experience and expertise are unrivaled. We build customized solutions to complex challenges by leveraging the latest technologies.

BRCK was initially made for the harshest environments on earth, now we’re bringing that same level of attention to the US market.

BRCK connects the unconnected by providing best-in-class reliability to ensure no business is left offline. With a team that built the VoIP industry, we deliver customized communication solutions and unparalleled support so customers worldwide can always count on their connection.

Let our team of VoIP pioneers build a customized communication solution to meet your unique business needs. With the best uptime and redundancy in the industry, we've got you covered.
